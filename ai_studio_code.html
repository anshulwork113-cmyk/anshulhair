My Story

When I was 20, I started noticing my hair thinning and losing density. Like many people, I tried almost everything—natural serums, hair growth shampoos, frequent dermatologist visits, and countless products that promised results.

Separated evidence-based facts from marketing myths.

Documented every step of the journey publicly.

Along the way, I documented everything I learned, separated evidence from myths, and built a framework that helped me better understand hair loss. Today, I share that journey so others can learn faster, avoid common mistakes, and feel less alone.
